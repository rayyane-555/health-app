Golden Retriever

Food

Golden Retrievers require a well-balanced diet tailored to their age and activity level

Puppies (2-6 months): 3-4 small meals, 2-3 cups/day

Juveniles (6-12 months): 3 meals, 3-4 cups/day

Adults (1+ years): 2 meals, 2-3.5 cups/day

Seniors: Adjust based on weight & activity

Feeding Tips:

✔️ Always provide fresh water

✔️ Limit treats to 10% of daily calories

✔️ Avoid artificial additives & fillers

✔️ Consult a vet for special dietary needs

Golden Retriever

Walks

Golden Retrievers are active and energetic dogs that need regular exercise to stay healthy and happy

Puppies (under 6 months): 5 minutes per month of age, twice a day (e.g., a 4-month-old puppy = 20 min walks)

Adults (1+ years): 1-2 hours per day, split into two walks

Seniors: Adjust based on energy and health, 30-60 minutes daily

Walking Tips:

✔️ Use a sturdy leash and harness for control

✔️ Avoid over-exercising puppies (their joints are still developing)

✔️ Mix it up with slow walks, brisk walks, and playtime

✔️ Dry completely, especially their undercoat, to prevent infections
